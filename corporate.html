SCIENCE BRIEF

Peer-Reviewed. Not Trend-Driven.

Every HARUKAZ program is grounded in published, peer-reviewed research. These are the studies we cite — and the reason we can say what we say with confidence.

Finding Study Key Result

L-Theanine & Attention

Journal of Clinical Medicine, 14(21), 7710 · 2025

 Mátyus et al. Meta-analysis of RCTs: L-theanine reduces reaction time and improves working memory accuracy. The compound found uniquely in high-grade matcha.

Matcha & Cognitive Function

PLOS ONE, 19, e0309287 · 2024

 Uchida et al. 12-month RCT: regular matcha consumption improves cognitive function and sleep quality in adults.

Mindfulness & Decision-Making

PLOS ONE · PMC6683193 · 2019

 Maddox et al. 16-week RCT (n=160): mindfulness practice significantly improves decision-making competence — the ability to make deliberate, high-quality choices under pressure.

Mindfulness & Executive Function

Nature Reviews Neuroscience, 16, 213–225 · 2015

 Tang, Hölzel & Posner Mindfulness practice physically reshapes the prefrontal cortex — improving executive function, impulse control, and cognitive performance at a neurological level.

Organizational Rituals & Performance

Davidson & McEwen (2012) · ResearchGate 2025

 Johnson & Johnson case 10-minute morning mindfulness rituals at Johnson & Johnson showed measurable prefrontal cortex activation and improved executive functioning across participating teams.
