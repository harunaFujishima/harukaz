AT-HOME EXPERIENCE

We Come to You.

A private matcha ceremony in your own home — no travel, no performance, no rush. Just ritual, presence, and a bowl prepared exactly for you.

◉ NORTH YORK  ·  LAWRENCE PARK  ·  FOREST HILL  ·  ROSEDALE
THE EXPERIENCE

Sixty Minutes.
Your Living Room.

We arrive with everything — ceremonial-grade Uji matcha from Kyoto, a handcrafted chawan, and a bamboo whisk prepared for the right temperature and texture. You provide only a quiet surface and your attention.

The session moves through three acts: a brief grounding practice, the careful preparation of your bowl, and a silent tasting with guided sensory reflection. No prior experience with tea or meditation is needed.

You leave with a small take-home portion of the matcha you tasted, and a printed tasting card to keep.

WHAT'S INCLUDED
  • 60-minute private ceremony at your home
  • Ceremonial-grade Uji matcha (Kyoto origin)
  • Hand-thrown chawan and bamboo chasen
  • Guided tasting and sensory reflection
  • Take-home matcha portion + printed tasting card
  • Service area: North York & surrounding neighbourhoods

QUESTIONS

Good to Know

Do I need any experience with matcha or meditation?

None at all. The session is designed to work for complete beginners. The only requirement is a willingness to slow down for an hour.

What do I need to prepare at home?

A quiet table or low surface, and roughly one square metre of clear space. We bring absolutely everything else — including a small mat if needed.

Which areas do you serve?

Currently: North York, Lawrence Park, Forest Hill, Rosedale, Leaside, and Bayview Village. Email us for other areas — we may be able to accommodate depending on distance.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes, with 48 hours' notice. Email info@harukazwellness.com and we will find another time that works.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are billed automatically on the same date each month. You can pause or cancel anytime by emailing us at least 5 days before your next billing date — no questions asked.
