ABOUT

Between Two Truths

The story of HARUKAZ begins not with matcha — but with the cost of never stopping.

FOUNDER'S STORY

I spent thirty years in Japan before relocating to Toronto in search of something I couldn't name at the time. A new chapter, perhaps. A different kind of performance. What I found instead was that I had brought everything with me — including the urgency.

Ten years in pharmaceutical sales taught me a great deal about the human body under pressure. I watched brilliant people degrade slowly. Not from illness, but from the relentless expenditure of attention without replenishment. I also watched what happened when the same people discovered a practice — any practice — that asked them to stop. Even briefly. The difference was not subtle.

I knew what a bowl of matcha could do. I had made it for myself every morning for fifteen years, the same way, with the same attention to each step. And I had noticed — long before I read the research — that the ten minutes it took changed the character of the hours that followed.

HARUKAZ was born between those two truths: what burnout costs, and what stillness returns.

THE PRACTICE

The Matcha Meditation™ Flow

Each step is intentional. Each transition is the practice.

1

Pause

A deliberate stop. You acknowledge that what comes next deserves your full presence.

2

Prepare

Setting out the bowl, the whisk, the scoop. Physical order creating psychological space.

3

Whisk

The ritual motion. Sixty seconds of attention to a single thing — the beginning of meditation.

4

Observe

The colour, the foam, the smell. Training your senses returns you to the present tense.

5

Sip

Slowly. The L-theanine enters your bloodstream. Alpha brainwave activity increases measurably.

6

Focus

You return to your work changed. The quality of your next hour reflects the quality of your pause.

VALUES

Three Principles. No Exceptions.

Evidence-Based

Every claim we make is traceable to published, peer-reviewed research. We do not offer wellness — we offer a practice with measurable neurological and cognitive outcomes. The science is in every session.

Authentically Japanese

We use single-origin matcha from specific farms in Japan. We use traditional tools: chasen, chawan, chashaku. We practise within a tradition that is over 500 years old — and we take that seriously. No approximations.

Radically Present

Our only product is your attention, returned to you. We do not sell relaxation or escapism or a lifestyle aesthetic. We offer a practice that puts you in full contact with your own mind — and then sends you back into the world more capable than you left it.

ORIGIN STORY

The Farmers of Uji, Kyoto

"The tea knows the season. Our job is simply to listen."
— Uji tea farmer, 4th generation

The tea gardens of Uji have been producing ceremonial-grade matcha for over 800 years. The process begins three weeks before harvest, when the plants are covered — slowly, carefully — to deprive them of direct sunlight. Deprived of light, the leaves produce more chlorophyll, more L-theanine, more of everything that makes high-grade matcha what it is.

The farmers who do this work are not suppliers. They are practitioners. Each batch they produce is the result of a year's observation — of soil, water, weather, and the subtle intelligence of plants that have been cultivated in the same soil for centuries.

When HARUKAZ sources from Uji, we are not purchasing a commodity. We are continuing a relationship between people who care deeply about a single, humble thing — and are willing to do it properly.
