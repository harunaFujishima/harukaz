Attention & Working Memory

L-theanine — the amino acid found uniquely in matcha — reduces reaction time and measurably improves working memory accuracy in controlled trials.

Mátyus et al. (2025). Meta-analysis of RCTs. Journal of Clinical Medicine, 14(21), 7710.

Decision-Making Competence

A 16-week RCT (n=160) found mindfulness practice significantly improves decision-making competence — the kind that separates reactive choices from deliberate ones.

Maddox et al. (2019). PLOS ONE. PMC6683193.

∞

Executive Function

Mindfulness practice physically reshapes the prefrontal cortex — the seat of executive function, impulse control, and long-range planning. The effects persist long after the session ends.

Tang, Hölzel & Posner (2015). Nature Reviews Neuroscience, 16, 213–225.

The sharpest minds in history have always sought stillness before action. The Stoics practised memento mori. The Samurai entered the tea house before battle. The most effective leaders of any era understood: performance begins in the pause.

HARUKAZ exists at this intersection — ancient Japanese ritual and the demands of the modern mind. We do not ask you to slow down. We ask you to stop — briefly, intentionally, completely — so that when you resume, you resume as a larger version of yourself.

This is not wellness as escape. This is wellness as strategy.
